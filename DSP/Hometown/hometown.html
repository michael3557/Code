---- Help -- Pages 1-9 ----

RandMan: Plugin Help: /help randman
LibsDisguises: Plugin Help: /help libsdisguises
Essentials: Plugin Help: /help essentials
EssentialsSpawn: Plugin Help: /help essentialsspawn
GriefPrevention: Plugin Help: /help griefprevention
ChestShopNotifier: Plugin Help: /help chestshopnotifier
/RandMan: Teleports player to a random position within the world border.
/libsdisguises: Main command for libsdisguises.
/disguise: Disguise yourself as an entity.
/disguiseentity: Disguise an entity as another entity.
/disguisehelp: Help command for LibsDisguises.
/disguiseplayer: Disguise another player as an entity.
/disguiseradius: Disguise all entities within a radius as an entity.
/undisguise: Undisguise yourself.
/undisguiseentity: Undisguise an entity.
/undisguiseplayer: Undisguise a player.
/undisguiseradius: Undisguise all entities within a radius.
/disguiseclone: Copy a disguise (or entity) and use it later.
/disguisemodify: Modify your own disguise
/disguisemodifyplayer: Modify the disguise of a player
/disguisemodifyradius: Modify disguises in a radius
/disguisemodifyentity: Modify a disguise by right clicking them
/afk: Marks you as away -from- keyboard.
/balance: States the current balance of a player.
/balancetop: Gets the top balance values.
/delhome: Removes a home.
/depth: States current depth, relative to sea level.
/getpos: Get your current coordinates or those of a player.
/help: Views a list of available commands.
/home: Teleport to your home.
/ignore: Ignore or unignore other players.
/itemdb: Searches for an item.
/list: List all online players.
/mail: Manages inter-player, intra-server mail.
/motd: Views the Message Of The Day.
/msg: Sends a private message to the specified player.
/pay: Pays another player from your balance.
/ping: Pong!
/realname: Displays the username of a user based on nick.
/recipe: Displays how to craft items.
/rules: Views the server rules.
/seen: Shows the last logout time of a player.
/sethome: Set your home to your current location.
/tpa: Request to teleport to the specified player.
/tpaccept: Accepts a teleport request.
/tpahere: Request that the specified player teleport to you.
/tpdeny: Reject a teleport request.
/warp: List all warps or warp to the specified location.
/spawn: Teleport to the spawnpoint.
/abandonclaim: Deletes a claim.
/abandontoplevelclaim: Deletes a claim and all its subdivisions.
/abandonallclaims: Deletes ALL your claims.
/trust: Grants a player full access to your claim(s).
/untrust: Revokes a player's access to your claim(s).
/containertrust: Grants a player access to your claim's containers, crops, animals, bed, buttons, and levers.
/accesstrust: Grants a player entry to your claim(s) and use of your bed.
/permissiontrust: Grants a player permission to grant his level of permission to others.
/subdivideclaims: Switches the shovel tool to subdivision mode, used to subdivide your claims.
/basicclaims: Switches the shovel tool back to basic claims mode.
/extendclaim: Resizes the land claim you're standing in by pushing or pulling its boundary in the direction you're facing.
/claim: Creates a land claim centered at your current location.
/buyclaimblocks: Purchases additional claim blocks with server money. Doesn't work on servers without a Vault-compatible economy plugin.
/sellclaimblocks: Sells your claim blocks for server money. Doesn't work on servers without a Vault-compatible economy plugin.
/trapped: Ejects you to nearby unclaimed land. Has a substantial cooldown period.
/trustlist: Lists permissions for the claim you're standing in.
/siege: Initiates a siege versus another player.
/unlockdrops: Allows other players to pick up the items you dropped when you died.
/claimslist: Lists information about a player's claim blocks and claims.
/claimexplosions: Toggles whether explosives may be used in a specific land claim.
/givepet: Allows a player to give away a pet he or she tamed.
/ignoreplayer: Ignores another player's chat messages.
/unignoreplayer: Unignores another player's chat messages.

---- Help -- Page 9/9 ----

/ignoredplayerlist: Lists the players you're ignoring in chat.
/csn: Main ChestShopNotifier command.

---- LWC ----

/cprivate: Create a private protection
/cpublic: Create a public protection
/cdonation: Create a donation chest
/cpassword: (Password) Create a password-protected protection
/cmodify: Modify an existing private protection
/cunlock: (Password) Unlock a password-protected block
/cinfo: View information on a protection
/climits: View the amount of protections you are allowed
/cremove: Remove a protection
/cremoveall: Remove all protections owned by the player
/lwc mode: (persist|droptransfer)
/lwc flag: (name) (on|off) Change protection flags
/lwc menu: (basic|advanced) Change menu styles
